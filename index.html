HOW IT WORKS

Let us show you the process of your dream trip coming true

Step 1: Select your travel prefrence

Choose:

  1. The date of your trip
  2. Your budget
  3. Flight duration that you prefer
  4. Weather that you prefer
  5. The purposes of this trip (Sightseeing, Food Travel or Shopping)

Step 2: The recommendation of your dream trip is generating

Our Story

Have you ever experienced a moment that you would like to travel during your vacation, but not sure about where to go? Whenever people are planning for a vacation online, the first question they always get is "Where to?" We figured there should be a platform for people who do not have a specific destination that they want to go, and here you have it — Itinerary helps you plan your trip no matter you have or have not a specific destination
